The Best Movies According to Jim

My top 3 movies of all-time.

We Were Soldiers

Great war movie. This is where courage and reality are blended to perfection.

Lord of the Rings Trilogy

Beautifully filmed and contains so many analogies describing the world we live in today can be drawn from it.

Master and Commander

A story desribing without exageration or excessive dramatics the comradarie and fellowship men can have for each other when facing a common goal and purpose.
